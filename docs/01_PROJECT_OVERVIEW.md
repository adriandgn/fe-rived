# **Project Overview: Rived**

## **Description**

"Rived" is an Upcycling Fashion Social Network. It connects creators (upcyclers) with consumers seeking inspiration or unique redesigned garments.

## **Core Value Proposition**

A visual-first platform where users can discover, filter, and interact with "Redesigns" (upcycled fashion items).

## **User Personas**

1. **Creators (Upcyclers):** Professionals or hobbyists who document their process, tools, and tips. They want to build a portfolio.  
2. **Consumers:** Users looking for inspiration, learning, or connecting with designers.

## **MVP Scope (Phase 1\)**

* **Authentication:** Signup/Login via Email/Password (Supabase).  
* **Public Access:** Unauthenticated users can view the full feed and details.  
* **Protected Actions:** Only authenticated users can Like, Comment, and Create Designs.  
* **Main Feature:** "Redesign" creation (Image upload \+ Metadata) and a Pinterest-style Masonry Feed.  
* **Search/Filter:** Advanced filtering by materials, categories, and tags (State in URL).
